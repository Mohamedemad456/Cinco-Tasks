Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TaskFlow

How do I get started with TaskFlow?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, create your first task, and start organizing your workflow. We offer a comprehensive onboarding process to help you get up and running quickly.

Is TaskFlow free to use?

Yes! TaskFlow offers a free tier with essential features for individual users. We also have premium plans with advanced features for teams and power users.

Can I use TaskFlow on my mobile device?

Absolutely! TaskFlow is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Access your tasks anywhere, anytime.

How secure is my data?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular security audits, and comply with industry standards to protect your data.

Do you offer team collaboration features?

Yes! Our premium plans include team workspaces, shared task lists, real-time collaboration, and advanced project management features.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 live chat support, comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and email support for all users.
